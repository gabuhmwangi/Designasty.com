ABOUT
executive summary about Designasty

Designasty limited is a recent(last spring)start-up manufacturer and sellers of all kinds of clothings mainly for people between 18 and 50 years.Not only is there a new look and design in different clothes but the fabric of each clothe has been improved to make the specific clothe comfortable to anyone wearing it.
Designasty clothing line sells classy,upscale, versatile expensive and high quality clothes. in the future Designasty hopes to sell clothes for children and old people.

company summary

Designasty's mission is is to sell a new look to its customers, based on style and quality. Any one wearing a Designasty product should feel confident and classy based on our products.

Designasty's strategy is to aggressively develop and market its line as an alternative to its competition and differentiate itself through its marketing strategies, exclusiveness and brand awareness.
